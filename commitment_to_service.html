Our Services

Our comprehensive 31 Point Inspection covers all crucial aspects of your home, ensuring everything is secure, functional, and well-maintained while you're away.

Exterior Checks

  • Check all exterior doors and windows for signs of damage or forced entry.
  • Verify that all doors and windows are securely locked.
  • Inspect the exterior for signs of water damage or leaks.
  • Check the roof for visible damage using binoculars or a drone.
  • Inspect gutters and downspouts for blockages.
  • Examine the yard and landscaping for any issues.
  • Verify that outdoor lighting is functional.
  • Check the mailbox and collect mail.
  • Inspect the driveway and walkways for cracks or damage.
  • Verify the security system is active and functioning.
  • Ensure outdoor furniture is in place and undamaged.
  • Check the pool and pool equipment for proper operation.
  • Inspect fences and gates for damage or needed repairs.
  • Verify that irrigation systems are functioning properly.
  • Inspect HVAC systems and ensure they are operational.
  • Check the thermostat settings.
  • Inspect the attic for signs of pests or leaks.

Interior Checks

  • Check all interior doors and windows for proper operation.
  • Ensure all appliances are working correctly.
  • Inspect plumbing fixtures for leaks or issues.
  • Verify that the water heater is functioning properly.
  • Check electrical outlets and ensure they are safe.
  • Test all light switches and fixtures.
  • Inspect the garage and ensure it is secure.
  • Check the condition of interior walls and ceilings.
  • Ensure there are no unusual odors inside the home.
  • Verify that emergency contact information is up to date.
  • Ensure smoke detectors are functioning.
  • Verify carbon monoxide detectors are operational.
  • Record the inspection findings and take photos as necessary.
  • Write a report summarizing the inspection and any actions taken.

Security and Trust

All payments are securely processed via invoices with enterprise-level encryption. Keys and passwords are stored securely in our safes, with encrypted codes corresponding to each key so no keys are labeled with the client name or address. This adds an extra layer of protection.

Our business is only owner-operated, meaning we are the ones taking care of your home, not an employee. We have background-checked ourselves via a third party and are licensed by the state of Florida to conduct business. We carry over $1 million in liability insurance.

We put your home and our business first, with no substitutions. Your home, and your satisfaction are our business. Details matter to us.
